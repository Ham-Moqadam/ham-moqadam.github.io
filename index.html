Bio

Ice Sheets | Radar | Scientific Machine Learning


I hold a PhD from the Alfred Wegener Institute, specializing in the application of radargram analysis and machine learning techniques to glaciological research.
With a strong background in scientific computing and geophysical data analysis, I use my expertise in radar remote sensing, deep learning, and geophysics to further our understanding of ice sheet dynamics.

I enjoy working at the intersection between glaciology and computer science.
